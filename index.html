FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What services do you offer? >

We offer barber services and spa treatments tailored to relaxation, styling, and personal care.

How do I book an appointment? >

You can book an appointment online through our website or call us directly. We also accept walk-ins subject to availability.

Do you offer gift cards? >

Yes, gift cards are available and make the perfect gift for anyone who loves beauty and relaxation.

What payment methods do you accept (cash, card, mobile money)? >

We accept cash, major cards, and mobile money (M‑Pesa). This makes it easy and convenient for all clients.

How can I reschedule or cancel my appointment? >

Simply call us or use our online booking system at least 24 hours before your appointment, and we’ll be happy to reschedule or cancel for you.
